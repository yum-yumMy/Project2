Cooking Tips & Tricks

Shut the stove off before
eggs are done

The trick to great eggs is to not overcook them! Whether you scramble, fry or poach, always turn off the stove a few minutes before the eggs look done even when they seem a bit runny. The remainder of the heat will cook them to fluffy perfection.

Use pasta water to create a pasta sauce

If you thought your pasta tasted good before, wait until you try this trick. The salty water will add more flavor to your dish. Reserved pasta water also contain starch that can be used to adjust the consistency of sauce.

Chop herbs with salt

To prevent herbs from flying all over the place when chopping, sprinkle a bit of salt onto the board. This will keep them in the place.
