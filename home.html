About

We welcome food lovers from all over the world especially those who love cooking and always want to try new recipes.
Malaysia, a melting pot of culture, with different races living harmoniously together, is one of the top food heaven in Asia.
Hence, this website named “Yum-MY” which is a wordplay on the words “yummy” and “MY” - the standard short form used for Malaysia - comes to life.


We intend to share Malaysian cuisine recipes - where the emphasis will be on three major cuisines which are Malay, Indian and Chinese Cuisine.
Occasionally, we also cover food from other races. We also covers specific recipes that are popular in certain holidays and events in Malaysia.
In addition, we also provide a few cooking tips and tricks to help people that find difficulty to cook Malaysian food.
On top of that, we also have our own rank of the best local products to help the audience to get a better understanding of each product from each brand.
